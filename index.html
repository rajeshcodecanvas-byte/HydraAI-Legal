Privacy Policy for HydraAI

Last Updated: April 2026

At HydraAI, your privacy is our top priority. This app is designed to work offline-first, ensuring your personal health data stays on your device.

1. Data Collection & Usage

Location Data: HydraAI requests access to your Coarse Location. This is used solely to fetch local weather data (temperature/heatwaves) to adjust your daily hydration goal. We do not track your movement or store your location history.

Physical Sensors: We use the Accelerometer (Tilt Sensor) to provide a visual sloshing effect in the water bottle UI. No sensor data is recorded or transmitted.

User Profile: Data such as your Name, Weight, and Gender are stored locally in a private SQLite database to calculate your health targets.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the Open-Meteo API to provide real-time weather adjustments. When fetching weather, only your general coordinates are sent to their server. No personal identifiers (Name, Email, Device ID) are shared.

3. Data Deletion & Control

You have full control over your data. You can use the "Reset App" feature in the Settings menu to permanently delete all local databases and preferences instantly.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions, contact us at: support@hydraai.com
