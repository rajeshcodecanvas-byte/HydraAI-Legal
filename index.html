Privacy Policy for HydraAI

Last Updated: April 2026

At HydraAI, your privacy is our top priority. Our application is designed with an offline-first philosophy, ensuring that your sensitive health and hydration data remains on your physical device and under your total control.

1. Data Collection & Usage

Location Data: HydraAI requests access to your Coarse Location (Approximate). This data is used exclusively to fetch local weather conditions (temperature and heatwave alerts) via the Open-Meteo API to dynamically adjust your daily hydration goal. We do not track your movements, store your location history, or share your coordinates with third parties for advertising.

Physical Sensors: We utilize the device Accelerometer (Tilt Sensor) to provide a premium, interactive visual "sloshing" effect in the water bottle UI. This data is processed in real-time on your device; no sensor data is recorded, logged, or transmitted off the device.

User Profile: Information such as your Name, Weight, and Gender are stored locally in a secure, private SQLite database. This information is used strictly for calculating physiological hydration targets.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the Open-Meteo API for real-time weather adjustments. When the app requests weather data, only approximate coordinates are sent. No personal identifiers (such as your Name, Email, or Device ID) are ever shared with this service.

3. Data Deletion & User Control

In compliance with global privacy standards and Google Play policies, we provide a "Reset App" feature within the Settings menu. Using this feature will instantly and permanently delete all local databases, user profiles, and preference files from your device.
