Privacy Policy for HydraAI

Last Updated: April 2026

At HydraAI, your privacy is our top priority. Our application is designed with an offline-first philosophy, ensuring that your sensitive health and hydration data remains on your physical device and under your total control. We do not require user accounts or cloud synchronization.

1. Data Collection & Usage

Location Data: HydraAI requests access to your Coarse Location (Approximate). This data is used exclusively to fetch local weather conditions via the Open-Meteo API to dynamically adjust your daily hydration goal. We do not track movements, store location history, or share coordinates with third parties for advertising.

Hydration & Profile Data: Information such as your Name, Weight, Gender, and Daily Hydration Logs are stored locally in a secure, private SQLite database on your device. This data is used strictly for calculating physiological hydration targets and displaying your personal history.

Physical Sensors: We utilize the device Accelerometer (Tilt Sensor) for real-time visual effects in the UI. This data is processed instantly; no sensor data is ever recorded or transmitted.

Notifications: We use local notifications to provide hydration reminders. Your reminder schedule is managed entirely on your device and is not shared with any external servers.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the Open-Meteo API for weather adjustments. When the app requests weather data, only approximate coordinates are sent. No personal identifiers (Name, Email, or Device ID) are ever shared with this or any other service.

3. Data Deletion & User Control

In compliance with Google Play’s Data Deletion policy, we provide a "Reset App" feature within the Settings menu. Using this feature will instantly and permanently delete all local databases, user profiles, hydration history, and preference files from your device. Since we do not store data in the cloud, this action is final and removes all traces of your data.
